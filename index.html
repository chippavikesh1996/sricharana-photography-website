“The only way to do great work is to love what you do.”

I found my love for art and the camera at a very young age. But first, like most Indian kids, I had to go through the ritual of engineering to appease my living Gods. After graduation, I couldn’t ignore the call of the lens for long. For over three years now, I’ve dedicated my time moulding myself into a professional visual artist I can be proud of. I’ve travelled across the country and beyond as a freelance, capturing stories that need to be told and places that need to be seen. I particularly love to shoot weddings because each occasion has over a thousand tales to tell, and I capture those moments of marriage one click at a time
